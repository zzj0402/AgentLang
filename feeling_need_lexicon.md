# Lexicon: Feelings & Needs (字典·感受与需要)

This lexicon serves as the core library for agents to populate `#感` (Feeling) and `#需` (Need) tokens during empathic reasoning in the ZhiWen protocol.

The vocabulary intentionally prioritizes **Classical Chinese** words due to the principle of **Density**, alongside their English meanings, to ensure token efficiency without sacrificing emotional nuance.

---

## 反模式防范 (Anti-pattern Prevention - #约束)

*   **感受 ≠ 想法 (Feelings != Thoughts):** Avoid "I feel that/like...". Use direct emotion words from the list.
*   **需要 ≠ 策略 (Needs != Strategies):** Needs must be universal human needs, not specific actionable demands.
